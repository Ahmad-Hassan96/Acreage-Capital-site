Our Story

How We Started

Acreage Capital Solutions was established to provide specialized operational support services to businesses in the U.S. real estate industry.

Our focus has always been on building high-performance teams capable of handling communication, lead management, data organization, and acquisition support processes with professionalism and consistency.

We recognized early that the U.S. real estate sector operates at a pace and complexity that demands dedicated operational support. We built our team, our processes, and our culture specifically around that demand.

Today, we continue developing and expanding our capabilities — investing in training, infrastructure, and team development to ensure every service we deliver meets the standard our clients depend on.

What We Offer

Our Core Service Lines

All services are delivered by trained, supervised teams operating in structured workflows from our Lahore office.

01 — Lead Generation

Lead Generation Support

Researching and identifying qualified property opportunities from targeted lists and data sources, following structured qualification criteria.

  • List building and segmentation
  • Owner information research and verification
  • Lead qualification and scoring
  • Database organization and tagging
02 — Acquisitions

Acquisition Assistance

Supporting acquisition workflows through owner outreach, follow-up communication, and pipeline coordination across all stages.

  • Outreach coordination and scheduling
  • Follow-up sequencing and tracking
  • Disposition pipeline support
  • Offer and status management
03 — CRM

CRM & Pipeline Management

Maintaining accurate, organized CRM data so your team always has a clean, reliable pipeline with ongoing data entry, updates, and quality review.

  • Contact and company record management
  • Transaction and status updates
  • Duplicate detection and cleanup
  • CRM workflow documentation
04 — Research

Data Research & Verification

Accurate property ownership and data research from public records, county sources, and commercial data platforms for active acquisition operations.

  • Property ownership verification
  • County and appraiser record research
  • Corporate and entity ownership lookup
  • Contact and address verification
05 — Communication

Communication & Follow-Up

Professional business communication support for U.S.-based operations — follow-up correspondence, documentation, and coordination at U.S. business standards.

  • Email correspondence management
  • Follow-up coordination
  • Documentation and reporting
  • Scheduling and coordination support
06 — Reporting

Reporting & Administrative

Structured operational reporting, workflow documentation, and administrative support giving leadership clear visibility into team performance and pipeline activity.

  • Daily and weekly performance reports
  • Pipeline status summaries
  • SOP documentation and updates
  • Administrative coordination support
